Major in Creative Technologies

The interdisciplinary Creative Technologies major emphasizes design and practice in the integration of digital technologies and the fine arts. In addition to foundational studay across the fine arts, the major provides training and experience across a range of creative/technical fields including video, sound, electronic music, music prudction, gaming, motion graphics, interactivity, AR/VR, UI/UX, mobile, web, and computer programming concepts.
